About Us

Welcome to Swipe4Paws! We’re a group of students from SDU in Sønderborg, bringing together animal shelters across Denmark to help people find their ideal pets with ease. Inspired by dating apps, we created a swipe feature that lets you explore and match with animals looking for a loving home.

Our Mission

We connect people with animals in need, making adoption fun and simple. Every profile brings you closer to a potential companion, and every swipe supports shelters across Denmark.

Our Unique Swipe Feature

Finding the perfect pet should be as easy and enjoyable as meeting a new friend. That’s why we designed a swipe feature, inspired by popular dating apps, that lets you explore pets at a glance. Swipe right to show interest in a pet, and swipe left to view other profiles. Each animal’s profile includes photos, personality details, and shelter information, so you can quickly get to know them.

Thank you for supporting our project and for considering adoption. Together, we can create more loving homes for animals in need!
